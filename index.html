About Me

Accounting roots,
data-driven mind.

I started my career as an accountant because I love the idea that every number tells a story. Five years and thousands of transactions later, I realised the story gets far more interesting when you bring data analytics into the room.

I have spent those years improving financial reporting accuracy by 45%, cutting invoice errors by 70% through automation, and managing over 2,000 monthly transactions with zero discrepancies. That foundation now sits underneath everything I do as a data and business analyst.

I am a Community Manager at Clarity Movement, where I help aspiring analysts in Nigeria find their footing in the data world. I am also a GCUB scholarship applicant, working toward a Master’s degree in Data Science to deepen my contribution to financial intelligence in emerging markets.
